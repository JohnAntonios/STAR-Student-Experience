Step Two: Arriving at Sydney airport.


When you land in Sydney, ensure you pick up all of your luggage;
please don't forget anything, as it will be difficult to recover!

A few things that you should do now:
  1. Connect to the Airport's free WiFi and contact your family to let them know you have arrived safely.
  2. Go to the currency exchange kiosk and the bank to convert your money into AUD currency and obtain your Australian bank, credit card.
  3. Go to the opal kiosk and collect your opal card! Be sure to topup your opal card, so that you can use it!
    Here is an instructional video on how to topup your opal card: Opal topup instructional video goes here.
  4. There are plenty of places inside the Airport to eat and drink. Be sure to rest before leaving the airport to get to your accomodation.

Getting to your accomodation.


From Sydney Airport, there are many options for transportation to your accomodation.

Be sure to install these following mobile apps, to help you with navigation and transportation advice!
Google Maps

Allows you to input your accomodation address, giving you options on how to get there via public transport!
Also gives you directions based on your current location, in case you get lost.

Opal

Allows you to input your accomodation address, giving you options on how to get there via public transport!
Also gives you directions based on your current location, in case you get lost.

TripView

Allows you to input your accomodation address, giving you options on how to get there via public transport!
Also gives you directions based on your current location, in case you get lost.

Please choose from below, if you need guidance on getting to your accomodation.

There are multiple methods of transportation, available for you to use:

  • Train
  • Bus
  • Uber / Taxi

If you ever feel in danger while en route to your accomodation,
do not be afraid to dial 000, which is the emergency number.

Follow all instrunctions carefully and make sure to find a buddy, if you are going with the University.

Enjoy your trip to your new home!
