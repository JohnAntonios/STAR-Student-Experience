Step Three: Arriving at accomodation.

Now it's time to unpack and unwind. You've had a long day and must be extremely tired.


Once you're ready, go out and explore the neighbourhood.
Explore your accomodation, and try to socialise with people who may be living in the same accomodation as you!


If you're living on campus, have a walk around the University and familiarise yourself with it, before your first day.

If you're not living on campus, no need to worry, simply sometime during the lead-up to the first day of University, make your trip to the Uni to understand how to get there and what method is the best for you!

This is a great opportunity for you to get comfortable with living in Sydney.
