Key Messages.

  • Prepare and educate yourself about Sydney and the University of Sydney before arriving.
  • Be sure to arrive early so you get the chance to adjust and become comfortable with living in Sydney.
  • There are always support services available to you, in case you require any kind of assistance.
    The Student Support centere is a great place to ask for help and guidance. It is located on level 3, Jane Foss Russell Building.
  • If you have any other enquiries or questions that this guide did not address, SydBot as a chatbot that you can speak to and it will help you out. You can learn more about SydBot and other FAQs here!
